Welcome to NewsRivr!

Hello and welcome to NewsRivr, glad you found us!

NewsRivr is a innovative way of reading your tweets in a 'River of News' and was invented by Dave Winer (1)
We try to give you our heart and soul implementation of this concept.

NewsRivr pulls in the relevant text and images from the page where the tweets links to, and displays them in the timeline. In that way a River of News tabs into the brain's excellent scanning capabilities. It allows you scan headlines and images for relevant information and immediately read them inline. This is a huge timesaver compared to the read, click on a link, wait and read again method of the normal timeline.
